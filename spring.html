Rhubarb shrub for an impressive martini

Used in the past as a method of preserving fruit, a shrub is just a fruit syrup made with vinegar instead of water. This tangy rhubarb shrub can be added to mineral water, sodas or cocktails for a refreshing tart drink. For a refreshing soda, add 1 oz shrub to tall glass. Fill with ice and top with sparkling soda water. Spike with vodka, gin or rum as you wish, or see below for the proportions for a rhubarb shrub martini.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups chopped rhubarb stalks
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 ½ cups apple cider vinegar
  • 1 oz rhubarb shrub
  • 1 oz vermouth
  • 1 oz gin
  • 2-3 ice cubes
  • ½ oz sparkling mineral water or soda water
  • Rhubarb stalk, for garnishing
